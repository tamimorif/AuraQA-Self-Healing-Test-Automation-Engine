===================================================================

AuraQA is a self-healing test automation engine. When a UiPath test fails due 
to a broken CSS/XPath selector, the system:

1. Detects the failure (UiPath Test Cloud)
2. Sends the failure payload to the Python/FastAPI backend

6. Provisions synthetic test data via TDO
